Bio

I am a Ph.D. student at the cluster of excellence "Reasonable AI", TU Darmstadt, working on self-supervised 3D understanding.

Previously, I worked with the Computer Vision Group and DeepScenario at the Technical University of Munich on unsupervised monocular visual odometry, and with Bosch Research on unsupervised online mapping for autonomous driving.

I received my M.Sc. in Robotics, Cognition, Intelligence from the Technical University of Munich and my B.Sc. in Vehicle Engineering from Central South University in China.
